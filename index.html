Calculator

History

The calculator as a computing device has a history of more than 300 years.

Throughout history, such devices have been created as:

  1. Summing machines that find the sum of numbers between the values entered and stored in the device.
  2. Tabulators that process information recorded on punched cards.
  3. Arithmometers designed to accurately perform simple arithmetic operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, division.
  4. Electronic calculators powered by electricity.
